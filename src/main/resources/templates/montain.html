Top Mountain Tourist Places

Explore breathtaking mountain destinations in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Telangana.

Maharashtra

Mahabaleshwar

Mahabaleshwar is a popular hill station in the Western Ghats known for its stunning views, cool climate, and lush greenery. Famous for its strawberries, it offers numerous viewpoints such as Arthur's Seat and Wilson Point.

Lonavala

Nestled between the Sahyadri hills, Lonavala is famous for its scenic beauty and numerous waterfalls. Don't miss the famous chikki and the breathtaking views from Tiger's Leap.

Khandala

A serene hill station, Khandala is known for its scenic viewpoints and lush green landscapes, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and peace seekers.

Matheran

Matheran is a charming hill station that is free of vehicles, making it perfect for tranquil walks amidst nature. Explore viewpoints like Panorama Point and Echo Point.

Amboli

Amboli is a lesser-known hill station in the Western Ghats, famous for its dense forests, waterfalls, and misty hills. It's an ideal spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Bhandardara

Known for its scenic beauty, Bhandardara is home to the famous Randha Falls and Arthur Lake. It's a great destination for camping and nature walks.

Panchgani

Panchgani is known for its educational institutions and strawberry farms. The Table Land is a highlight, offering stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Malshej Ghat

A picturesque mountain pass, Malshej Ghat is famous for its waterfalls, wildlife, and lush green hills. It's an ideal spot for trekking and photography.

Kalsubai Peak

As the highest peak in Maharashtra, Kalsubai is perfect for trekking enthusiasts. The view from the top is breathtaking, especially at sunrise.

Karnala Fort

Karnala Fort is a historic fort surrounded by beautiful landscapes and rich biodiversity. It's an excellent spot for hiking and exploring ancient architecture.

Karnataka

Coorg (Kodagu)

Coorg is known for its coffee plantations, stunning landscapes, and rich culture. Don't miss the Abbey Falls and Dubare Elephant Camp.

Chikmagalur

Famous for its coffee estates and breathtaking views, Chikmagalur offers numerous trekking trails and scenic spots like Mullayanagiri.

Mullayanagiri

As the highest peak in Karnataka, Mullayanagiri is a trekker's paradise with stunning views and challenging trails.

Nandi Hills

A popular getaway near Bangalore, Nandi Hills is known for its sunrise views, cool climate, and historical significance.

Agumbe

Known as the 'Cherrapunji of the South', Agumbe is famous for its rainforests, diverse flora and fauna, and stunning sunsets.

Kudremukh

A mountain range and popular trekking destination, Kudremukh offers breathtaking views and rich biodiversity.

Sakleshpur

Known for its coffee plantations and picturesque hills, Sakleshpur is ideal for nature walks and adventure activities.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi is famous for its stunning ruins, ancient temples, and unique rock formations.

Murudeshwar

Home to the world's second tallest statue of Lord Shiva, Murudeshwar is also known for its beautiful beaches and temples.

Telangana

Hyderabad (Golkonda Fort)

Golkonda Fort is a historic fortress known for its architecture and rich history. Explore its numerous gates, temples, and the stunning view from the top.

Warangal (Fort and Lakes)

Famous for its historic fort and beautiful lakes, Warangal is a city steeped in history and natural beauty.

Khammam (Kinnerasani Wildlife Sanctuary)

This wildlife sanctuary is known for its diverse flora and fauna, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Nalgonda (Bhongir Fort)

Bhongir Fort offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and is an excellent spot for history buffs and trekkers.

Medak (Medak Fort)

Medak Fort is known for its impressive structure and the beautiful Medak Church, one of the largest in Asia.

Siddipet (Bhavanasi Hill)

Bhavanasi Hill is a serene spot offering beautiful views and is perfect for a quiet getaway.
